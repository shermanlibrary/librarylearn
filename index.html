LibraryLearn is simply a blown-out WordPress theme designed to put a roof over videos made by libraries. It makes managing this kind of complicated content a little easier on staff and presents patrons with a usable and accessible landing-spot.

It's device agnostic, light weight, and future-friendly - and it's totally yours if you're willing to wait!

This theme was made and first goes live for the Nova Southeastern University Libraries in January. A version for the library community will later drop at the Distance Library Services Conference in April 2014.

If you like, we're happy to keep you in the_loop.
